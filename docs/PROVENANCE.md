# Provenance

Version 2.0.0 starts from the version-1 exact release and adds a newly exactified full word-length-four upper certificate, a strengthened manuscript, direct dimension-499 reconstruction, explicit exception-based verification, and expanded adversarial tests.

The prior exact certificates and finite tensor strategies are retained unchanged except that the dimension-499 compact comparison target is sharpened to `25087538451/100000000000`; its exact strategy value is unchanged and exceeds the new target by exact cross multiplication.

Numerical discovery and exactification are outside the trusted base. The trusted verifiers regenerate the symmetry bases and recompute every headline mathematical acceptance condition. The archived payload in `untrusted_generators/` exists to reproduce the serialized proof object byte-for-byte, not to establish the theorem.

Input hashes are recorded in `SOURCE_HASHES.txt`. Legacy executable serialization is not included and is never loaded by either verifier.
